Best time to visit

Shortest daily wait times are between 8-10am or after 10pm.

Accessibility

The Empire State Building is fully ADA compliant, with handicapped restrooms on the 86th Floor Observatory and lowered viewing walls and binoculars. Service dogs are allowed throughout the building. Motorized and non-motorized wheelchairs are permitted. The 86th Floor has ramps to make getting around easier and lowered viewing walls so that visitors in chairs can still take in the view.

What's Included

The CityPASS ticket experience

CityPASS includes general admission entry to the 86th floor observatory of this New York City icon, plus bonus same-night general admission; enjoy a same-day second visit (May-August between 10pm-closing; September-April between 8pm-closing).

Offers & Upgrades

On-site attraction enhancements
102nd Floor Observatory

Available at Observatory ticket office (2nd floor) or 86th floor kiosk. Cost: $20.

Express Pass

Purchase from an official Empire State Building employee at the onsite ticket office on the day of arrival to move to the front of each line. Cost: $33. Please note: Street sales agents outside of the building are not employed by the Empire State Building Observatory and are not authorized to sell Express upgrades.
